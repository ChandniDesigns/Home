Hello! I'm Chandni

As a designer with a coding background, I'm passionate about creating engaging and user-centric experiences that help businesses succeed.

My journey into the world of UX/UI started at a small startup, where I cut my teeth as a website designer. As I grew in my career, I discovered a deep passion for user research, design processes, and creating innovative solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

One of my favorite things to do when I'm not designing is to explore the great outdoors. Whether it's a hike in the mountains or a leisurely stroll through a local park, I love to immerse myself in nature and soak up all the inspiration it has to offer. There's just something about the fresh air and the greenery that always leaves me feeling energized and inspired.

Another passion of mine is exploring different cultures and cuisines. As an Indian, I'm proud to come from a country with such a rich and diverse culinary heritage. From the spices of the south to the chaat of the north, there's always something new and exciting to try. Whether I'm traveling abroad or simply checking out a new restaurant in town, I love to immerse myself in new flavors and experiences that expand my horizons and fuel my creativity.

All in all, I'm truly grateful for the journey that's led me to where I am today. As I continue to grow and evolve as a designer, I'm excited to see where my passion for UX/UI will take me next.
